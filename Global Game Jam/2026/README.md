## 📖 Overview

&gt; *"Who looks outside, dreams; who looks inside, awakes."*
&gt; — **Carl Jung**

**Speculum Animae** (Latin: *"Mirror of the Soul"*) is an introspective browser experience that invites you to embark on a profound journey of self-discovery. Through **15 carefully crafted philosophical stages**, you'll explore the depths of consciousness, identity, and human existence — guided by the wisdom of history's greatest thinkers.

This is **not a game** in the traditional sense. It is a **mirror**. It asks questions without easy answers. It listens without judgment. And it transforms as you reveal yourself to it.

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | **Enter the mirror** — Open your mind and browser |
| 2 | **Receive the question** — Each stage presents a philosophical inquiry (narrated aloud) |
| 3 | **Write your truth** — Reflect in the text area; there are no wrong answers |
| 4 | **Witness transformation** — Watch the mirror's layers peel away as you progress |
| 5 | **Complete the journey** — Discover what remains when all masks fall |

### Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `Enter` | Submit reflection |
| `Shift + Enter` | New line |
| `Space` | Contemplate the mirror |
| `Click mirror` | Reveal deeper layers |

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### Why Text & Voice?

We chose **writing input + voice output** deliberately:

| Choice | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Writing** | Slows thought, enabling deeper introspection |
| **Voice** | Creates intimacy, as if the mirror itself speaks |
| **No microphone** | Fully private, nothing recorded |
| **Browser-based** | Accessible to all, no installation needed |
